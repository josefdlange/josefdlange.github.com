Welcome to my personal page. My name is Josef David Lange. I'm currently a student of Computer Science and Theatre Arts at the University of Puget Sound.

Here I hope to share my ideas, my problems (at least the technical ones), and my solutions. It's also a good place to centralize ways to communicate with me. Here and there I might add something fun, like some delicious food (well, not to eat, but at least instructions on how to make it).

I am currently working on the following projects:

  • LendingShack, a web application designed to encourage a strong community through the lending and borrowing of property.
  • Boot, an application for Mac OS X that allows users to quickly restart onto other startup disks on their Mac.
  • Propr, a web application for managing stage props in a theatre environment.
