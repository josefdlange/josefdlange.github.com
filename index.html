Welcome to my personal page. My name is Josef David Lange. I'm currently a student of Computer Science and Theatre Arts at the University of Puget Sound. I'm a child of the 1990s but I won't bore you with stories about how the SNES was the best console of all time or how Power Rangers changed my life (it was and they did).

Here I hope to share my ideas, my problems (at least the technical ones), and my solutions. It's also a good place to centralize ways to communicate with me. Here and there I might add something fun.

I am currently working on the following projects:

  • LendingShack, a web application designed to encourage a strong community through the lending and borrowing of property.
  • Boot, an application for Mac OS X that allows users to quickly restart onto other startup disks on their Mac.
  • Propr, a web application for managing stage props in a theatre environment.
